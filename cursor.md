# Cursor Project Context: On-Call CTO

## 1. Vision & Identity
- **Brand Name:** On-Call CTO
- **Tagline:** Macao’s Part-Time CTO & Technical Fixer.
- **Model:** "Fractional CTO" / CTO-as-a-Service. 
- **The Core Offer:** 1 Day per Week (In-Office in Macao) for a fixed retainer of 10,000 MOP/month.
- **Positioning:** We aren't consultants selling slide decks; we are senior engineers who sit in your office and fix your technical debt on the spot.

## 2. The Refined "Digital Growth Ladder" (The Offer)
The site must reflect these three distinct steps:

### Step 1: Technical Relief (The Emergency / "The Doctor")
- **Hook:** "Stop the bleeding."
- **Scope:** Fixing slow WordPress sites, SAP/API hanging issues, broken forms, and repairing blind Meta/Google Pixel/CAPI tracking. Making the existing engine work as intended.

### Step 2: Data Architecture (The Infrastructure / "The Pipes")
- **Hook:** "Build the brain."
- **Scope:** Cleaning historical data, setting up automated pipelines to collect customer data, CRM hygiene, and cross-platform integration. Ensuring data is a reliable asset.

### Step 3: Operational AI (The Leverage / "The Scaling")
- **Hook:** "Scale the output."
- **Scope:** Deploying custom AI agents that use the clean data from Step 2 to qualify leads, handle support, or automate internal workflows.

## 3. Technical Stack (Next.js 14)
- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router)
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4 (Global styles in `app/globals.css`)
- **Icons:** Lucide-React
- **Design Vibe:** Technical, clean, minimalist, high-trust. Use "Glass" navigation, grid backgrounds, and code snippets to project engineering authority.
